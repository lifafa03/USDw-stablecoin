## How Your System Solves This

### Problem 1: No Geography
```
Farmer: Lives 50km from nearest bank branch
        Would take full day of work to visit
        
Your system: Works anywhere with phone
            Instant verification
            24/7 availability
```

### Problem 2: No Proof of Income
```
Street vendor: Makes $5/day but has no W-2 form
              Bank says: "No documentation, no loan"
              
Your system: Uses transaction history
            Mobile money = proof
            ZK proofs = verify without exposure
```

### Problem 3: No Minimum Balance
```
Daily worker: Has $10-30 cash at a time
            Bank minimum: $50
            Result: Can't open account
            
Your system: $0 minimum
            Micro-transactions supported
            Works for everyone
```

### Problem 4: High Fees
```
Poor person: Makes $5/day
            Bank fee: $3 withdrawal
            That's 60% of daily income in fees!
            
Your system: ~$0.01 per transaction
            99% savings
            Accessible to poorest
```

### Problem 5: No Credit History
```
Young person: Has never borrowed before
             Bank says: "No credit history"
             Catch-22: Need credit to build credit
             
Your system: Use mobile money history
            Alternative credit scoring
            Build credit from transactions
```

### Problem 6: Predatory Lending
```
Desperate person: Takes payday loan
                 400% APR interest
                 Borrows $500, pays back $650 in 2 weeks
                 Never escapes debt trap
                 
Your system: Fair rates (8-15%)
            Objective algorithms
            Can actually repay
            Build wealth not debt
```
