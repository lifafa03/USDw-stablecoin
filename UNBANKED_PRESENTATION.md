## Your Solution (2 minutes)

### How Your System Fixes This

**Problem 1: No Geography**
```
Traditional: Need to visit bank branch
Your System: Verify from anywhere with a phone
```

**Problem 2: No Credit History**
```
Traditional: Need 5-year history (don't have)
Your System: Use mobile money history (they have this!)
```

**Problem 3: No Proof of Income**
```
Traditional: Need W-2 form (informal workers don't have)
Your System: Use transaction history (M-Pesa, GCash)
```

**Problem 4: No Minimum Balance**
```
Traditional: $50-100 minimum (they don't have)
Your System: $0 minimum (blockchain)
```

**Problem 5: High Fees**
```
Traditional: $10/month + per-transaction fees
Your System: ~$0.01 per transaction (blockchain)
```

**Problem 6: Slow Decisions**
```
Traditional: 2-5 days to approve
Your System: Instant (ZK cryptographic proof)
```

**Problem 7: Privacy**
```
Traditional: Full financial history exposed
Your System: Only threshold proven (privacy preserved)
```

### Your Three-Part Solution

**Part 1: Stablecoin (USDw)**
- Stores value without currency volatility
- Works globally (not just USD)
- Accessible to anyone with phone

**Part 2: Credit Scoring**
- Objective algorithm (no discrimination)
- Fair evaluation (multiple factors)
- Risk-based pricing (not predatory)

**Part 3: ZK Proofs**
- Prove claims without exposing data
- Privacy preserved
- Instant verification

## Questions You Might Get

### Q: "Aren't there already solutions for the unbanked?"
A: "Yes, microfinance institutions exist, but they're slow (2-5 days), expensive ($50+ per loan), local only, and subjective. Blockchain enables instant, free, global, objective solutions. That's the innovation."

### Q: "How do you prevent fraud?"
A: "Smart contracts are transparent and auditable. Nullifiers prevent proof reuse. Transactions are immutable on blockchain. Plus, low amounts ($200-500 average) make fraud less attractive than traditional banking."

A: "Smartphone adoption is 77% in Kenya, 73% in Philippines, 68% in India. Growing rapidly. For those without phones, offline USSD codes work. But this is already 80% of target market."
